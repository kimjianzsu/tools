Unmount VOB

To deactivate a ClearCase VOB on your computer, click ClearCase > Unmount VOB. After the VOB is unmounted, its folder no longer appears under the dynamic views you have started on your computer.

This option becomes active after you connect to ClearCase. This option is available only on Windows platforms and is not available in ClearCase LT.
