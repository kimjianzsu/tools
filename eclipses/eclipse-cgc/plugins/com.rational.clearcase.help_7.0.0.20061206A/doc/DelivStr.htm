Deliver Stream

To start or continue a Rational ClearCase UCM deliver operation, which makes changes in your  development stream available to the rest of the project team, click ClearCase > Deliver Stream or click the ClearCase Deliver Stream icon.

This option becomes active after you connect to ClearCase.
