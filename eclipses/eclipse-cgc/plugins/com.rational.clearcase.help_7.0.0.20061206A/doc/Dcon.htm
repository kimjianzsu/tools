Disconnect from ClearCase

In some circumstances, such as in the case of mobile computing, you may want to disconnect from ClearCase and from your network, before reconnecting.

To disconnect from ClearCase:

    Click ClearCase > Connect to Rational ClearCase. 

When you have disconnected from ClearCase, the check mark next to the option name disappears and the other ClearCase menu options become inactive.

Note: When you reconnect to ClearCase after disconnecting from the network, it may take several minutes before you can proceed with ClearCase operations.

This option becomes active after you connect to ClearCase.
