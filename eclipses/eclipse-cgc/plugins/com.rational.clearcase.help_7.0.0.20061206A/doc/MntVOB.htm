Mount VOB

To activate a VOB on your computer and work with its contents in a dynamic view, click ClearCase > Mount VOB in the Rational Developer product. After the VOB is activated, its folder appears under every dynamic view that is started on your computer.

This option becomes active after you connect to ClearCase. It is available only on Windows platforms and is not available in ClearCase LT.
