Share a Rational Developer project 

Before you can access ClearCase options in the Team context menu for a Rational Developer project, you must share the project in Rational ClearCase.

To share a Rational Developer project:

  1. Right-click on the project in the Rational Developer product and click Team > Share Project.
  2. In the Share Project wizard, select ClearCase SCM Adapter and click Next.
  3. Following the instructions in the Share Project wizard to complete the process of sharing the project.

After you share the project, you can select it and access ClearCase options from the Team context menu. Other users who import the project can also access these options.
